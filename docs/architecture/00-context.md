## Business problem

People consume news from a single national bubble. They miss how the same global event is framed differently in other countries. The platform surfaces that divergence in a live dashboard.

## Stakeholders

- **Course assessors** (Prof. Mueller): grade the report and presentation.
- **End users** (hypothetical): journalists, researchers, curious readers comparing national framings.
- **Team**: Jack (EA), Nadi (DE), Karina (Business).

## In scope

- Five countries: DE, US, IT, MM, KZ.
- Article-level ingestion, classification, and visualisation.
- 6-week rolling corpus for the prototype; longer historical via GDELT.

## Out of scope

- Real-time push notifications.
- User accounts, personalisation.
- Native mobile apps.
- Multi-language NLP fine-tuning (use off-the-shelf multilingual models).
